BACKGROUND

I am a student-athlete pursuing a bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering with a minor in Business at the University of Michigan. I focus the majority of my time on both schooling and my team, the Michigan Dance Team. I am very adamant about receiving a strong, undergraduate education in order to lead me on to my future career and education. Additionally, I try to take advantage of every opportunity presented to me that involves field experience, as I feel that I learn best when executing hands-on tasks.



I am a hungry, willing, and determined learner when it comes to growing myself as an individual. In engineering, I seek out the most valuable opportunities that will help me hone my knowledge and expand my skills as an engineer. I am a very task-oriented individual and am motivated by challenges and far-fetched outcomes in all aspects of life. I enjoy serving as a leader to others and recognizing the potential of myself and my peers around me.

Outside of school and dance I love spending time with my friends and family. I enjoy taking trips to places I've never traveled to before and exploring what new adventures are out there. I also love reading, discovering new music, binge-watching movies/series, and playing card games. I am a sucker for sun and warm weather, too, so when summer comes around, I am definitely outdoors lying on a beach or floating in a boat as much as possible.
